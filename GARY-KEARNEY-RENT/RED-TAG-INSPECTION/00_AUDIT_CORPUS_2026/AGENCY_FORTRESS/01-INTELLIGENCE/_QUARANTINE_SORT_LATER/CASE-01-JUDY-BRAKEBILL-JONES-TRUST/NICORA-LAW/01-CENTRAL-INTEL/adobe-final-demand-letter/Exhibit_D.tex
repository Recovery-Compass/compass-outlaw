{\Large\bfseries DECLARATION OF NUHA SAYEGH}

\vspace{1em}

\noindent I, Nuha Sayegh, declare:

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*., leftmargin=0.5in]
    \item I am over the age of 18 and competent to testify to the matters stated herein. I have personal knowledge of the facts set forth in this declaration.

    \item On May 19, 2025, I was present at a family meeting at the estate property located at 17742 Berta Canyon Road, Salinas, California. The meeting was attended by Eric Jones, Heidi Marichen Jones Blanchard, and Gretchen Angela Jones.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}

\noindent\textcolor{NavyHeader}{\rule{\textwidth}{1pt}}\\[0.3em]
{\large\bfseries\textcolor{NavyHeader}{I. AUTHORIZATION TO DOCUMENT THE MEETING}}\\[0.3em]
\noindent\textcolor{NavyHeader}{\rule{\textwidth}{1pt}}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*., leftmargin=0.5in]
    \item At the commencement of this meeting, Heidi Blanchard explicitly requested that I serve as the record keeper for the discussion. Heidi Blanchard asked me directly: \textbf{``Nuha, are you writing notes? I think it would be helpful.''}

    \item Based on this direct request from the adverse party, I took detailed, contemporaneous notes of the proceedings. The statements detailed below are from the written notes that Heidi Blanchard requested I create.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}

% ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
% SECTION II

\noindent\textcolor{NavyHeader}{\rule{\textwidth}{1pt}}\\[0.3em]
{\large\bfseries\textcolor{NavyHeader}{II. ADMISSION OF EXECUTOR STATUS}}\\[0.3em]
\noindent\textcolor{NavyHeader}{\rule{\textwidth}{1pt}}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*., leftmargin=0.5in]
    \item During the meeting, Eric Jones asked: ``Mom chose me to be the executor. Is that a fact?'' I witnessed Heidi Blanchard respond affirmatively: \textbf{``Yeah, okay.''}

    \item Immediately following this acknowledgement, Heidi Blanchard instructed Eric Jones to execute his duties. She stated: \textbf{``So the first thing that you need to do as the executor, you need to start the probate process.''}
\end{enumerate}

{\large\bfseries\textcolor{NavyHeader}{III. EVIDENCE OF BAD FAITH ASSET CONVERSION}}\\[0.3em]
\noindent\textcolor{NavyHeader}{\rule{\textwidth}{1pt}}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*., leftmargin=0.5in]
    \item During this meeting, Heidi Blanchard admitted to listing the Trust's 2013 Mercedes-Benz for sale on Craigslist for \textbf{\$11,000}.

    \item Crucially, Heidi Blanchard acknowledged that she lacked the authority to sell the vehicle without the Trustee's consent. She stated to Eric Jones: \textbf{``I don't know if you, if you don't agree, we can take it off.''}
\end{enumerate}
