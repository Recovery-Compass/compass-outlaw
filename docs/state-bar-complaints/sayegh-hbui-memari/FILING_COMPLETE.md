## Violations Documented

### 6 Failures Per Attorney

#### Failure #1: Material Error in Financial Filing
- **What:** Filed FL-150 showing $5,500 monthly income
- **Truth:** Nuha had $0 employment income (on EBT/food stamps)
- **Impact:** Court calculated support on false income figure
- **Rule Violation:** 1.1 (Competence)

#### Failure #2: Late Transmission of Remote Hearing Login
- **What:** Sent LA Court Connect login at 8:39 AM
- **Problem:** Hearing started at 8:30 AM (9 minutes late)
- **Impact:** Nuha couldn't access remote portal on time
- **Rule Violation:** 1.3 (Diligence), 1.4 (Communication)

#### Failure #3: Failure to Advise on Remote Testimony Restrictions
- **What:** Didn't warn remote appearance bars live testimony
- **Impact:** Judge ruled Nuha couldn't testify at her own hearing
- **Result:** Silenced during critical custody hearing
- **Rule Violation:** 1.4 (Communication)

#### Failure #4: Failure to Present Critical Safety Evidence
- **What:** 600+ file evidence dossier not effectively presented
- **Includes:** Drug incident with 13-year-old daughter
- **Impact:** Children remain without supervised visitation
- **Rule Violation:** 1.3 (Diligence)

#### Failure #5: Abandonment
- **When:** November 21, 2025 (48 hours after hearing)
- **Reason:** "Breakdown in attorney-client relationship"
- **False Basis:** Email sent by third party without authorization
- **Response:** "My office will NOT be [reinstating]" despite clarification
- **Retained:** $4,530 of original $7,500 retainer
- **Rule Violation:** 1.16 (Termination)

#### Failure #6: Conflict of Interest - Chatsworth Scheduling Priority
